$$由于 \qquad A_{12}=W_{1}-W_{2} \quad A_{12}=q\int^{q_{2}}_{q_{1}}\overrightarrow{E} \cdot d \overrightarrow{l} =q(\phi_{1}-\phi_{2}) $$
$$\tag{点电荷的电势能}因此 \qquad W = q \phi$$
